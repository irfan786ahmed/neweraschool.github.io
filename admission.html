ADMISSION PROCESS

For admission to any class the child’s registration is to be done at the first instance. At the time of registration the date of interaction and written test is announced. For the admission in class I, student’s interaction takes place and the list of selected students is displayed on the School Notice Board.

Similarly, For the student’s of classes II onward, a merit list is prepared on the basis of their written test and list of selected candidates is displayed on the School’s Notice Board. Parents of selected students are asked to fill in the admission form and deposit the admission fee and other charges by the prescribed date. Registration to a particular class is not a guarantee of admission.

Class I - IX

Class 1-child should be of complete 5 years by 1st April of the year in which the admission is sought, and so on. A student who is seeking admission to the school is registered only after the completed registration form is submitted to the school. The school reserves the right to admit students who are found fit for admission. In all matters related to admissions, the management’s decisions are final. Pupils who have attended another school cannot be admitted until a transfer certificate from the earlier school is produced. A student who is admitted to the school and all admission formalities are complete, is only refunded the caution fee if he or she wants to withdraw from the school Withdrawal notice must be given within 30 days of last date of just completed term. No refund of fees will be made for withdrawal during term.
