About Us

NEW ERA INTERNATIONAL School Makrana, established in 1992 is owned and managed by the leading businessman, Mr. Jameel Gesawat. NEIS aims at imparting quality education to the budding youngsters of our society by providing value based education that creates confident, competent and morally mature individuals who will always be a credit to the society at large.

Chairman's Message

New Era International School aims to provide a conducive atmosphere which would enable each child to realize his potential and his latent talents. The objective is to ensure the holistic development of the student with particular emphasis on maintaining high academic standards. The school instils in each child a desire for excellence, secular ethos, care for the less privileged, love for adventure and sensitivity towards the environment.

Principals's Message

Sited at the Educational region of Makrana in Rajasthan, New Era International School is an oasis which unfolds its wealth to a magnificent world of knowledge. The School is affiliated with RBSE and has all the modern amenities that makes it a fully fledged Public School. The curriculum of the school is designed to develop the multifarious creativity of the child. A wide range of multi-dimensional activities are also organized.
